About Me

I am a Chilean researcher living in Switzerland holding a resident permit B with right to work. I have 8+ years of experience in topics that include data science, data analysis, Python, SQL, machine and deep learning. I am LSST Data Science Fellowship alumni, I won Hack’n’Lead 2023 and a STAR Prize in 2025. I am analytical, structured, and a curious team player with a passion for learning and solving complex problems.
