About Me

I am a Chilean postdoctoral researcher and science communicator. I am currently working at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland. I got my Ph.D. in astrophysics at Universidad de Valparaíso, Chile in 2018. I am an alumna of the LSSTC Data Science Fellowship Program (2nd cohort).

My research focuses on understanding the Universe through the gravitational lensing effect. My current project involves searching for new gravitational lens systems using artificial intelligence — with the goal of preparing for the data influx from LSST and the Euclid mission.
